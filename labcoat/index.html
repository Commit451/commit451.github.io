Features

Everything you need from GitLab, on the go

Projects

Browse projects, view READMEs with full markdown rendering, explore file trees, and star your favorites.

Issues

Create, browse, and manage issues. Filter by assignee, label, or milestone. Comment and update on the go.

Merge Requests

Review merge requests, view diffs, check approvals, and stay on top of your code review queue.

Pipelines

Monitor CI/CD pipeline status, view job logs, and catch failures before they become problems.

Push Notifications

Get notified about activity on your projects. Tap a notification and jump straight to the relevant screen.

Self-Hosted Support

Works with GitLab.com and self-hosted GitLab instances. Your server, your data, your rules.

Open Source

Built in the open, for the community

LabCoat is and always will be free and open source. Built with Kotlin Multiplatform, contributions are welcome. Check out the source, file issues, or submit a merge request.
