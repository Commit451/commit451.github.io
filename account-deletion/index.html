Account Deletion

Last updated: March 11, 2026

How to Request Deletion

To request deletion of your account and associated personal data, email us at support@commit451.com.

What to Include

  • The email address associated with your account
  • The app or service name
  • Any additional details that help us identify your account

What Happens Next

  • We may ask you to verify account ownership before processing deletion.
  • Once verified, we will delete or anonymize personal data associated with your account, subject to legal and operational requirements.
  • Some records may be retained where required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or backup recovery windows.

Processing Timeline

We aim to process verified deletion requests within 30 days.

Need Help?

If you have questions about deletion or data handling, contact us at support@commit451.com.
